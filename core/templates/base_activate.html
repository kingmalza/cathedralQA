When to register
The first time you try to access a new Cathedral environment, you need to register your license to activate the product.

Registering your copy of Cathedral is very simple; If you do not have the activation key, simply select the REGISTER NEW LICENSE button and fill in the few fields on the registration form.
How to access
Once the product has been registered you will receive an email containing the license key to be used for activation via the activation form on this page, once the product is activated you will be able to log in the first time using the user and password also present in the email of registration
